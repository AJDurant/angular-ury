Listen

There are many ways to listen to URY, whether you're a York student, friends and family of our presenters, or just interested in what student radio has to offer.

On Air

AM Radio

We are broadcasting on AM radio all across campus on 1350KHz Medium Wave.

So what are you waiting for? Ask your parents if you can have their old boom box, and listen like it's 1989.

On Campus

URY can occasionally be heard at campus events such as move-in days, Freshers' Fair, Roses, and Candidate Interview Night. Keep an ear out!
